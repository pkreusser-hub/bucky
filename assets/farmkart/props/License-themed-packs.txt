FARM KART — THEMED PROP PACKS: LICENSES & ATTRIBUTION
=====================================================
Farm Kart is a private, non-commercial family project (never published/sold), so
CC0, CC-BY, and CC-BY-NC assets are all acceptable. Attribution is recorded below as
good hygiene even where not strictly required. Every model was verified against its
source page's stated license before inclusion.

Prefixes: city_ = CITY, snow_ = SNOW, jgl_ = JUNGLE, west_ = WESTERN.
